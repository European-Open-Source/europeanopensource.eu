Our Vision

We want building a platform that unites the technological realities of the European Union and promotes an open, accessible, and collaborative technological ecosystem.

Our Mission

To create a hub for EU Member States, where it's possible to:

  • Research and exchange innovative ideas
  • Share and adopt open-source projects
  • Support the development of transparent and secure technologies
  • Build a collaborative network between developers, businesses, and institutions

Our Values

  • Open Source First We promote the development, adoption, and use of open-source software in every technological field
  • Collaboration We believe in cooperation between individuals, businesses, and institutions to create a robust and secure technological ecosystem
  • Transparency Every project must be clear and documented, ensuring traceability and openness
  • Inclusivity We welcome anyone who wants to contribute, regardless of background or experience
  • Security and Digital Sovereignty The EU must be independent from non-European proprietary technologies and ensure the protection of its citizens' data
